Japanese Curry

An assortment of vegetables and meat boiled to perfection and then added with curry roux cubes to turn the water into a delicious sauce.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Curry Roux Cubes
  • Pork

Steps

  1. Cut every ingredient into a cube so it can easily boil inside the pot.
  2. Boil everything in water until all of it is soft.
  3. Lastly add the curry cubes and stir until it turns into a sauce.
  4. Optionally add more water to change the viscosity of the sauce.
  5. Add seasonings like salt, pepper, and sugar, for more flavor if desired.
