Tonkatsu

Pork covered in flour and bread crumbs deep fried till its soft in the inside and crispy on the outside.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut pork into strips.
  2. Season pork with salt & pepper.
  3. Cover the pork with flour.
  4. Get the yolk of the egg use it for dipping the flour covered pork.
  5. After the pork is soaked in the yolk, cover it with bread crumbs.
  6. Then deep fry it.
